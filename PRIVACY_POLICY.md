# Privacy Policy for SähköVahti

**Last Updated: October 27, 2025**

## Introduction

SähköVahti ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data Collected by the App

- **Electricity Price Data**: The app fetches public electricity spot prices from external APIs
- **Local Storage**: Price data is cached locally on your device for offline access and performance
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Technical Data**: The app may automatically collect device information such as:
  - Device model and operating system version
  - App version
  - Network connection type (for data fetching)

## How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:
- Displaying current and historical electricity prices
- Providing price predictions and analysis
- Calculating cheapest electricity periods
- Caching data for offline use and better performance
- Updating the home screen widget

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Cached price data is automatically deleted after 7 days
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- No user accounts or cloud storage are used

## Third-Party Services

### Data Sources
The app fetches data from the following external sources:
- GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) - For electricity price data and predictions
- These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies

### Advertising
The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process the following information:
- **Advertising ID**: A unique, user-resettable ID for advertising
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, screen size
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, ad performance metrics
- **Location Data**: Approximate location for ad targeting (if permitted)

**Ad Personalization**:
- You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads)
- Non-personalized ads will still be shown
- For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Data Collection by AdMob**:
- AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC
- AdMob's use of your data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- You can learn more about how Google uses data at [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

## Data Sharing

- We do not share, sell, or rent your information to third parties
- No personal data is collected or shared
- Price data fetched from public APIs is for display purposes only

## Children's Privacy

SähköVahti does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences interested in electricity pricing information.

## Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:
- Use the app without providing any personal information
- Clear cached data at any time by uninstalling the app
- Stop the app from accessing the internet by revoking network permissions

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of the app:
- All data fetching uses secure HTTPS connections
- Local data is stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms
- No sensitive personal information is collected or stored

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

## Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection to:
- Fetch current electricity prices
- Download price predictions
- Update cached data

You can use cached data offline for up to 7 days.
